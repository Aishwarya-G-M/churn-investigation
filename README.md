Distributed Systems Coursework
- As a part of the Master Distributed Systems coursework project, I work on the churn investigation in Peer to Peer systems.
- One of the ways to classify Distributed Systems is Client-Server and Peer-to-Peer. Peer-to-peer systems are those that do not exhibit or showcase the master slave nature.
- In such systems, there are multiple ways an overall concensus is reached because such systems are continuously prone to nodes dynamically leaving the system and joining.
- This paper demonstrates the effects of such dynamics in the system.
